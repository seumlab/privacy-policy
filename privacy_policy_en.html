Privacy Policy

Seum Digital Co., Ltd. (“the Company”) values users’ personal information and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act and related laws.

This privacy policy informs users how their personal data is collected, used, and stored.

1. Personal Information Collected

The Company may collect the following personal information:

  • ① When using Google login:
    - Email address (for Google Calendar integration and identification)
    - Google Calendar event ID (to synchronize schedules and prevent duplication)
  • ② When using app features:
    - Schedule title, content, start/end date, time
    - Importance setting, notification settings
  • ③ When using weather features:
    - Location information (device GPS coordinates)
  • ④ When using notifications:
    - Notification time settings per schedule (stored locally on the device)

※ Information is collected only when the corresponding features are used, and some items are optional.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • Schedule synchronization via Google Calendar
  • User schedule management and notifications
  • Display schedules based on weather information
  • Maintain personalized feature settings

3. Retention and Use Period

- Personal information is deleted immediately after the purpose of collection/use is fulfilled.
- If the user deletes the app or unlinks their Google account, the related information is also deleted.

4. Provision to Third Parties

The Company does not share personal information with external parties, except:

  • When prior consent is obtained from the user
  • When required by law

5. Entrustment of Processing

The Company does not entrust personal information processing to third parties.

6. User Rights and Exercise Methods

  • Unlink Google account
  • Delete or modify schedules within the app
  • Delete information by deleting the app

※ For inquiries, please contact us via email below.

7. Use of Automatic Collection Tools

- The Company does not use cookies or similar tools.
- Some authentication data may be processed automatically when using Google APIs (in accordance with Google's privacy policy).

8. Technical and Administrative Measures

  • Schedule data is stored locally on the user’s device and not transmitted externally.
  • Tokens issued for Google sync are securely stored and not used for other services.
  • Notification features are handled via the device’s internal alarm system.
